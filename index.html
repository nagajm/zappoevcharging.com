From charger handshake to billing to settlement — Zappo is the full stack. Transparent at every step.

OCPP 2.0.1 Native

Built on the open standard. Any OCPP-compliant charger joins the network — no proprietary lock-in, ever.

Live Operator Dashboard

Every active session, live kWh, revenue, and station health — in real time. Nothing hidden, nothing guessed.
